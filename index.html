Questions

Before you ask.

Will it damage my engine?

No. Flame Link works entirely in the exhaust, downstream of your engine — it never touches your internals, timing or factory tune. How your car drives doesn't change.

Is it safe?

It's built around safety: hard burn-time limits, a hardware watchdog and fused power, and it cuts every output the instant something's off. High-grade, heat-rated parts and a sealed gasoline feed — no open fuel.

What fuel does it use?

A small, sealed gasoline feed. The lines stay closed — nothing leaks, drips or pools, on a hot exhaust or anywhere else.

Will it flame on its own, or catch fire?

Only when you trigger it — a lift-off, a downshift, or the app. Parked with the engine off it's completely inert; there's no open flame sitting there waiting.

Which cars does it fit?

BMW F & G series, with a plug-in harness made for them. Not sure about yours? Drop your model in the order form and we'll confirm.

Can I control how big the flames are?

Yes. Tune flame size, intensity and crackle from the app, save your setups and switch on the fly — from a subtle daily crackle to full send.

Is it legal?

Off-road and show use only. A flame kit may be illegal on public roads where you live — check your local laws and run it responsibly.

How is it installed?

It ships with the wiring harness and a step-by-step guide. Want it done for you? Professional installation by our team is GH₵ 2,000 — add it in the order form.
