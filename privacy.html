Privacy Policy

CM Performance · Last updated June 2026

What we collect

When you place an order through our website, we collect your name, email address, phone number, car model, and any notes you provide. This information is used solely to process and fulfil your order.

How we use it

Your information is used to contact you about your order, confirm your build, arrange delivery or installation, and provide customer support. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except as required to deliver your order.

Email OTP

We use Supabase's built-in email service to send a one-time passcode to verify your email address. No third-party email provider is involved unless we later add one; at that point this policy will be updated.

Cloudflare Turnstile

This site uses Cloudflare Turnstile to help prevent automated bot submissions. Turnstile collects certain device and browser information for security purposes. Use of Turnstile is governed by Cloudflare's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service. Cloudflare's Turnstile Privacy Addendum applies to your use of this widget.

Data retention

We retain order information for the duration of our business relationship and as long as necessary for warranty, support, and legal purposes. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us.
